************
Introduction
************
Gaming is more engaging when play with other people. Brainztorm Social module 
provide mechanisms to the players to interact with people in Social Networks, 
share their milestones and compete with friends in leaderboards.

Brainztorm Social also makes possible to have an authentication method in 
which players are familiarized and allows you get the user profile data, like id, 
name and avatar image. Internally, Brainztorm Social uses the `Unity Social`_ 
functionallity to acomplish some tasks.
