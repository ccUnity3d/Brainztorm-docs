RateGame

************
Introduction
************
Positive ratings and comments are very important for your game success. So, making an easy 
way to bring the users to the proper place to leave their feedback is a task you shouldn't 
forget. The RateGame module consist in a dialog prompt your users to rate your game in the 
correspondant store (App Store, Google Play etc) for other users see their opinions 
and ratings about your game.
